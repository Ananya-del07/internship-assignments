Prompts and Comparison:
1. Resume Writing
Weak Prompt:
Write a resume.
Strong Prompt:
Create a professional one-page resume for a final-year Computer Science student with skills in Python, Machine Learning, and NLP. Include education, projects, skills, and internships.
Comparison:
The weak prompt generated a very general resume without specific details or structure. The strong prompt produced a well-organized resume with relevant sections, proper formatting, and detailed content based on the given skills.
2. Business Idea Generation
Weak Prompt:
Give me a business idea.
Strong Prompt:
Suggest 2 low-investment business ideas for college students in India. Include target audience, required resources, and estimated cost.
Comparison:
The weak prompt resulted in broad and generic ideas. The strong prompt provided practical and detailed ideas with clear explanations, making them more useful and realistic.
3. Study Plan
Weak Prompt:
Make a study plan.
Strong Prompt:
Create a 7-day study plan for Data Structures covering topics like arrays, stacks, queues, and trees. Include daily schedule and revision.
Comparison:
The weak prompt produced a basic and unclear plan. The strong prompt generated a structured and detailed schedule with proper topic distribution and time management.
