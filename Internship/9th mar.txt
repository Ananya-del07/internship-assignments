Assignment Name: House Price Predictor
Explanation
A House Price Predictor uses Linear Regression, a Machine Learning algorithm used to predict continuous values. In this assignment, a simple dataset containing house sizes and their corresponding prices is used to train the model.
The model learns the relationship between house size (input feature) and house price (output label). After training, the model can predict the price of a house when a new house size is given as input.
For example, if the model is trained with data showing that larger houses usually cost more, it can estimate the price of a house of a given size.
