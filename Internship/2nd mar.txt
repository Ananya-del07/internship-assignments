Assignment Name: ML Idea Generator

1. College – Student Performance Prediction

Machine learning can analyze student study patterns and past academic records to predict exam performance.
Input → Output
Attendance, study hours, previous marks → Predicted exam score or grade.

2. College – Student Dropout Prediction

ML models can identify students who may drop out based on academic and behavioral data.
Input → Output
Attendance, grades, participation → Dropout risk prediction.

3. College – Course Recommendation System

ML can suggest suitable courses for students based on their interests and previous subjects.
Input → Output
Student interests, past course grades → Recommended courses.

4. Healthcare – Disease Prediction System

Machine learning can analyze patient data to predict possible diseases at an early stage.
Input → Output
Symptoms, age, medical history → Predicted disease.

5. Healthcare – Heart Disease Risk Prediction

ML models can estimate the risk of heart disease using patient health indicators.
Input → Output
Blood pressure, cholesterol, age → Heart disease risk level.

6. Healthcare – Medical Image Detection

ML can analyze medical images such as X-rays or scans to detect abnormalities.
Input → Output
Medical images (X-ray, MRI) → Detected disease or abnormality.

7. Shopping – Product Recommendation System

ML can recommend products to customers based on their browsing and purchase history.
Input → Output
Search history, previous purchases → Recommended products.

8. Shopping – Customer Purchase Prediction

ML models can predict whether a customer will buy a product based on browsing behavior.
Input → Output
Browsing time, product views → Purchase prediction.

9. Shopping – Price Prediction System

ML can predict the best price for a product based on demand and market trends.
Input → Output
Demand data, previous prices → Predicted product price.

10. Shopping – Customer Review Sentiment Analysis

ML can analyze customer reviews to understand whether feedback is positive or negative.
Input → Output
Customer reviews or comments → Sentiment (Positive/Negative).
